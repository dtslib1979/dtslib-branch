Core Principles

Philosophy

The foundation of DTSLIB thinking

The moment you speak, the system has failed.
Build architectures that make words unnecessary.

I

Reverse Orality

Speak first, structure later. Voice input as primary creative medium. Conversation logs become raw intelligence for systematic refinement.

II

Protocol Thinking

Design systems, not solutions. Every action becomes a reusable protocol. Patterns over instances, frameworks over features.

III

Mobile Sovereignty

The smartphone is the complete creative environment. No desktop dependency. PWA-first architecture enables true location independence.

IV

Conversation as Source

AI dialogue generates intellectual capital. 8,760+ hours of conversation become searchable, refinable, publishable assets.
